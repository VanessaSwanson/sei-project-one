Seinfeld Frogger

How to play

Get your George Costanzas safely to the other side of the road before the timer runs out.

Avoid the irksome characters blocking his way, and then hop on buses to reach safety.

If you can grab a coffee on the way, you'll pick up extra points - serenity now!

Once you enter the game, click on the start button to begin.

Good luck!

Countdown: 60
